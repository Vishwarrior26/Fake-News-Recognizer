The Problem of Fake News

That’s Fake! is a browser extension which helps prevent the spread of misinformation. The extension will look at the site to tell you how accurate the information is. That’s Fake! will be useful to anybody on the internet and especially to those who frequently read their news on their laptop since 52% of Americans say they see fake news online regularly. That’s Fake! is easy to use and only needs one click to see if an article is accurate.
